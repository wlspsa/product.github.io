Cat In Box Co.

At Cat In Box, we facilitate the inevitable: a well-socialized cat and the box it was always meant to inhabit. Each pairing is deliberate, vetted, and emotionally final. The cat has chosen the box. The box has accepted.

What you receive:

A cat in excellent health and questionable priorities
A box that immediately becomes the cat’s home, office, and entire belief system
An operation grounded in ethical care, radical transparency, and the quiet understanding that this was always going to happen

No tricks. No turbulence. Just cats fulfilling their ancient purpose: occupying boxes that are slightly too small and staring at you as if this was your idea.

Curated Box Alignment

Our team studies each cat’s size, temperament, and metaphysical stubbornness to determine the box it will accept without protest. This is not guesswork. Incorrect boxes are quietly rejected by the cat and removed from consideration.

Pre-Acceptance Conditioning

Before placement, each cat is gently introduced to the concept of the box. By the time you meet them, the cat has already decided the box is correct and is no longer interested in alternative housing, including your furniture.

Ethical Care & Radical Transparency

All cats receive proper veterinary care, enrichment, and human interaction. We are unusually honest about what you are buying: a healthy cat who will ignore expensive items in favor of cardboard. There are no surprises, only confirmation.

Post-Arrival Adjustment Support

If your cat temporarily exits the box, do not panic. Our guidance helps you understand when this is normal, when it is ceremonial, and when the cat is simply reminding you that the box still exists and remains important.
